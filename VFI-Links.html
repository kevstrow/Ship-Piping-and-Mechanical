Vendor Furnished Information (VFI)

VFI - Overview

Vendor Furnished Information (VFI) is the document and data information that the shipyards need as input to engineering and design products.

VFI is used for creating library part models for the 3D design and for purchasing items that have been recommended by designers.

ALL items used to build ship systems require VFI. Its importance cannot be understated.

How do you get started?

You are ready to look for VFI when you have completed the following:

  • Equipment sizing calculations (HP, KW, or other)
  • Pipe diameter sizing (for valves, fittings, and strainers)
  • Ship spec and applicable regulatory review

VFI Tips

You can refer to similar ships to confirm sizing calculations and for equipment manufacturer recommendations.

2D and 3D CAD drawings can be found on various websites by performing an internet search with the keywords, "engine CAD drawing," or equivalent.

PDF drawings can be converted to CAD with a free conversion program or imported into CAD and traced.
